Privacy Policy for Infinite Scroll Pro

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Infinite Scroll Pro ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Shopify app.

Information We Collect

Data We DO NOT Collect

Infinite Scroll Pro is a theme extension that runs entirely in the customer's browser. We do NOT collect, store, or process:

  • ❌ Personal customer information (names, emails, addresses)
  • ❌ Shopping cart data
  • ❌ Purchase history
  • ❌ Payment information
  • ❌ Browsing behavior or analytics
  • ❌ Cookies or tracking data

Data We DO Collect

The only data we access:

  • ✅ Theme Configuration: Settings you configure in the Shopify theme editor (button text, colors, preferences)
  • ✅ Installation Data: Basic app installation status to enable the extension

Storage Location: All settings are stored by Shopify in your theme configuration. We do not maintain separate databases.

How We Use Information

The theme configuration data is used solely to:

  1. Display the infinite scroll feature according to your preferences
  2. Apply your chosen styling (colors, button text)
  3. Enable/disable auto-scroll or manual button mode

Data Sharing

We do NOT share any data with third parties. Period.

Data Retention

  • Theme Settings: Retained as long as the app block is installed in your theme
  • Upon Uninstall: All settings are automatically removed when you remove the app block from your theme
